---
title: Grandma's Chili Sauce
tags: main dish
source: Grandma Irvin
language: en
ingredients: |-
  1 28-oz. cans tomatoes
  ¾ cup white sugar
  ¾ cup vinegar
  1 small onion, chopped
  2 teaspoons salt
  1 teaspoon ground cloves
  1 teaspoon pepper
  1 teaspoon cinnamon
---

Mix all ingredients in a small pot. Boil until darkened and bit thicker (about 10-15 minutes on simmer). Can be thickened more with 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 1 tablespoon water added to the simmering mixture.
