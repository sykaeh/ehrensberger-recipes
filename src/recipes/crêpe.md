---
title: Crêpe
tags: breakfast
language: en
ingredients: |-
  1 ½ cups flour
  1 tsp baking powder
  1 tsp salt
  1l milk
  4 eggs
  50g butter or margarine, melted
---

Mix flour, baking powder, salt. Then add milk and eggs. At the end add melted butter or margarine. Cook in oiled pan for a few minutes and turn.
